FIRST

Your body has stopped generating new blood vessels in your intimate area. These blood vessels are essential for your sexual function, as they are the exclusive pathways that deliver blood to this region. A reduction in these pathways results in diminished blood flow, leading to less satisfying sexual experiences.

SECOND

Micro-plaque has begun to accumulate within these blood vessels, a condition known as atherosclerosis. As you age, this build-up of micro-plaque within your vessels continues, acting like a barrier that restricts blood flow. The more micro-plaque builds up, the less blood can pass through. As a result, women may start to notice a decrease in their sexual satisfaction and function. Ultimately, this can impact sexual health.

Frequently asked questions

What is GAINSWave® for Her?

GAINSWave® for Her is a non-invasive medical procedure designed to improve sexual health in women.

How does it work?

GAINSWave® uses shockwave therapy to stimulate blood vessel growth and enhance blood flow to the intimate area, improving sexual function.

Is GAINSWave® safe?

Yes, GAINSWave® is considered a safe and effective procedure for women’s sexual health. You can find clinical studies and research here.

Does it require anesthesia?

No, GAINSWave® is typically painless and does not require anesthesia or any downtime.

How long does a session take?

A typical GAINSWave® session lasts no more than 30 minutes.

>Is there any downtime after a treatment?

No, there is no downtime after a GAINSWave® treatment, and you can resume your daily activities immediately.

When can I expect to see results from GAINSWave®?

Some women may notice improvements after just a few sessions, but the full results may take a few weeks.

How many treatments are required?
Depending on your goals, anywhere from 6 - 12 treatments. We also have a preventative maintenance and performance program to maintain results and optimize performance.
Are the results permanent?

While GAINSWave® can provide long-lasting results, periodic maintenance sessions may be recommended.

Are there any side effects associated with GAINSWave®?

GAINSWave® is generally well-tolerated, and side effects are minimal, such as mild discomfort or temporary redness.

Can I have GAINSWave® if I have a medical condition?

It's important to consult with a healthcare provider to determine if GAINSWave® is appropriate, especially if you have underlying medical conditions.

How much does it cost?
Treatment cost varies depending upon each patient's actual needs. Extensive plaque buildup can require multiple sessions. A typical session costs $500 and full treatment can typically be financed for as low $69 per month.
