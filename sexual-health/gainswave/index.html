FAQ's

Is shockwave therapy for sexual health safe?

Yes, shockwave therapy for sexual health is considered safe and non-invasive. It's performed by trained professionals with specialized equipment.

How long does a session typically last?

A session usually takes around 15-20 minutes, making it a convenient option for individuals with busy schedules.

Does shockwave therapy hurt?

Patients generally describe the sensation as mild discomfort or a tingling feeling. However, it is typically well-tolerated.

Are there any contraindications or groups of people who should avoid shockwave therapy?

Individuals with certain medical conditions or contraindications should consult with their healthcare provider before undergoing shockwave therapy. These conditions may include pregnancy, blood clotting disorders, or specific medications. Its essential to discuss your medical history with your healthcare provider before treatment.

The number of sessions required can vary depending on individual needs and the specific condition being treated. Typically, a series of sessions is recommended for optimal results.
